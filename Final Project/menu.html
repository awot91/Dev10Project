Arancini di Riso

Mama Mia! This is an italian street food classic. These little guys are rice balls stuffed with cheese, rolled in bread crumbs, and, you guessed it, DEEP FRIED. Savory, Crunchy, and Cheesey all rolled into one. Comes five to an order. Served with a side of marinara sauce.

Barbacoa Tacos

¡A Dios Mio! This is the God Father of Mexican classics. Slow cooked barbacoa style beef, nestled inside two double-stacked corn tortillas. Topped with cilantro and diced onions. Comes with a side of rice and refried beans. Ask for our house-made green salsa!
15.00

Taiyaki

Itadakimasu! Dig into this Japanese favorite street sweet. A cross between the crispyness of a waffle and the soft,moist texture of a cake. These pastries are filled with traditional red bean paste or nutella.

Chicago-Style Hotdog

Hot diggity dog! Nothing is more American than a hot dog. We use 100% premium beef hotdogs topped with yellow mustard, bright green relish, onions, tomato wedges, pickle spear, sport peppers and a dash of celery salt all in a steamed poppy seed bun. Served with a side of fries.

Vada Pav

Swaagat to India! These are Mumbai's most famous street food. Three deep fried spicy potato balls, smoothered in dry garlic coconut chutney, the green chutney and the sweet tamarind chutney served in steamed buns. Packed with flavor and also vegan friendly.
8.00
